# Focus Pantry Product Strategy

Focus Pantry helps busy solo cooks turn pantry odds and ends into a calm 3-day meal plan before food goes bad. It is for time-poor home cooks with half-used ingredients and decision fatigue; it is not for macro trackers, chef hobbyists, or grocery-first recipe browsers.

Activation: enter five available ingredients and get tonight's meal plus a 3-day leftover-aware plan in under two minutes.
Core loop: cook from pantry, mark what was used, save what worked, and regenerate the next plan from remaining ingredients.
Retention: saved preferences, avoided-waste history, and pantry depletion make the fifth plan smarter than the first.
Money path: Pro unlocks saved meal plans, grocery export, and seasonal pantry packs; the boundary is evidenced locally as a concept only.
Revenue/taste tradeoff: monetization stays behind planning extras so basic food-waste reduction remains trustworthy.
